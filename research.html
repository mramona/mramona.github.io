Research Interests

My research interests are in the area of wireless sensor networks.

For my PhD project I am particularly interested in analyzing and predicting the impact of the environment on WSNs at physical and routing layer.

Traces used in my reasearch work are from winter and summer in-field experimental campaigns performed on Mount Bondone, near Trento.
Physical layer traces were/are collected using TRIDENT, a tool developed in our group for the execution of communication tests without the need for a tethered infrastructure.
